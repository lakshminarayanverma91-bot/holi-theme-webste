🌸 Ancient Roots

What is Holi?

Holi is the ancient Hindu spring festival of colors, celebrated across India, Nepal, and South Asian communities worldwide. Rooted in mythology, harvest, and the divine love of Radha and Krishna — it dissolves every social boundary for two days of pure joy.

Streets transform into living canvases of pink, yellow, and blue. Strangers become friends. Grudges are washed away by color. For one magical day, all are equal.
